We do not collect any personal information from users directly. However, the app does process images of exam papers, which may contain personal information related to the students' performance.

The information processed by the app, such as the images of exam papers, is used solely for the purpose of analyzing and counting marks related to each topic. The results are then used to generate an Excel file for each student. This data is not stored or transmitted by the app.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data processed by the app is stored locally on the user's device. We do not store or transmit any data to external servers. We take reasonable measures to protect the data on your device, but we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not share any information processed by the app with third parties. The app is designed to function entirely offline, ensuring that your data remains private and secure.

Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no specific user rights regarding data access, correction, or deletion. All data remains on the user's device and can be deleted by the user at any time.
